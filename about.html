About MACEV

Massive Co., Ltd. aims to provide new technology development and related services to completed vehicle manufacturers that have established core competencies in design, engineering, development, and production supervision. The company specializes in completed vehicle assembly production, frame/component production, mold/injection production, 3D production/sample assembly.

  • 2020. 3 - 5

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Establishment of the corporation for the purpose of electric vehicle development and sales. Participation in the development of MASTA HIM for KAAON EV R&D LTD.

  • 2021. 4 - 5

    Initiation of the development

    Initiation of the development of overseas exhibition-model electric personal mobility for bicycles. Completion of the prototype sample in August. Initiation of the development of compact electric road cleaning vehicle. Completion of the prototype sample in August.

  • 2021. 8 - 11

    Introduction to Expos

    Introduction of compact electric road cleaning vehicles and small electric personal mobility at the Jeju International Electric Vehicle Expo, the Daegu International Future Auto & Mobility Expo, and the Gwangju Green Car Korea

  • 2022. 6 - 10

    Initiation of the development

    . Initiation of the development of Korean-style compact electric road disinfection, cleaning vehicle, personal mobility, and manhole cover dirt removing vehicle.
