Road Disinfection Vehicle

The Road Disinfection Vehicle represents a groundbreaking solution at the forefront of modern sanitation technology. Designed to combat the challenges posed by unyielding contaminants, this vehicle combines cutting-edge disinfection capabilities with efficient road cleaning functionalities.

Dual-Seater Capability: Addressing the practical challenge of an individual driver being unable to operate the vehicle for extended hours, our road disinfection vehicle offers seamless driver rotation. Moreover, it facilitates direct spraying through a hose by the co-passenger seated alongside for areas requiring misting. Additionally, for the implementation of senior job projects, a supplementary assistant's presence is deemed essential.

Compact Design: With dimensions slightly exceeding those of an ultra-compact 1-seater road sweeper, this vehicle grants the flexibility to access narrow alleys, traditional markets, and similar locales.

Integrated Sanitization Functionality: Pioneering a groundbreaking feature, our road disinfection vehicle boasts a cutting-edge disinfection system, allowing simultaneous execution of disinfection and cleaning tasks.

Uninterrupted Operations: By necessitating mere water replenishment during operations, our vehicle consistently generates the required disinfectant, ensuring uninterrupted misting capabilities.

Utility Task Vehicle

In response to pressing environmental concerns and evolving regulatory demands, we proudly present our latest innovation in the realm of transportation - the electric Utility Task Vehicle (e-UTV). This eco-friendly and advanced mobility solution is driven by electric motors and fortified with lithium iron phosphate batteries, offering exceptional safety and heightened efficiency.

In response to the mounting global environmental challenges and the heightened focus on international environmental regulations, the imperative to pursue electrification in the realm of transportation has become undeniable. This paradigm shift involves the substitution of conventional internal combustion engines with electric motors and advanced battery technologies, giving rise to electric vehicles (e-vehicles). Positioned as an environmentally friendly alternative, e-vehicles offer the commendable advantage of displacing fossil fuels, thus contributing to sustainable mobility practices while mitigating ecological impacts, including reduced noise and vibration.

In this context, our initiative centers on the development of a cutting-edge military-grade electric Utility Task Vehicle (e-UTV) equipped with lithium iron phosphate batteries, esteemed for their superior safety attributes and minimal fire risk compared to conventional lithium-ion counterparts. Accompanying this groundbreaking vehicle, we are concurrently working on auxiliary batteries and portable charging solutions, designed to optimize the performance and efficiency of the eco-friendly military e-UTV.

The hallmark of our approach lies in harnessing the inherent adaptability of ultra-compact electric vehicles, empowering us to create an open multi-platform architecture suitable for challenging terrains. This innovative design ethos enables silent operation while affording the capability to accommodate diverse equipment and weaponry, thus catering to the unique demands of tactical and strategic military applications.

Aligned with the highest standards of technological excellence and environmental stewardship, our vision is anchored in realizing the tangible benefits of this environmentally conscious military e-UTV, bolstered by superior performance and an unwavering commitment to sustainability.
